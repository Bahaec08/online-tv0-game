How to Play T·V·0

Guess the opponent's secret 3-digit number. The number has no leading zero and all digits are unique.

T = right digit, right place
V = right digit, wrong place
0 = none of the digits are correct

Use the digit eliminator to keep track of digits you know are not in the secret.
